Welcome to storefully!

storefully is an application created to help track various types of media you have in your bookshelves. So far, it can keep track of your books, albums, discs (like DVDs and Blu-Rays), and games. Further improvements will be made in time and pushed as often as they can be.

Please note the navigation at the top that will take you through the various lists (currently own and a wishlist), and through the types of media on your current list. You can also add in new media or update existing media.
